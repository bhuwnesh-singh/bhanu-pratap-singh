Alarming conditions : difficulty breathing or shortness of breath, chest pain or pressure, loss of speech or movement,

Covid-19 Preventions

1. Wash your hands regularly with soap or clean with alcohol-based hand rub.
2. Maintain at least 1m distance between you and others.
3. Avoid touching your face
4. Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.
5. Stay home if you feel unwell and refrain from activities that weaken the lungs.
